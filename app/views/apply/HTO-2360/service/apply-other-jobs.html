Using this service to apply for your grant

Civil service employment

If you are a civil servant, your employer will provide support instead of Access to Work. You must contact your employer so they can assess and arrange the support you need.

Access to Work can cover mental health support for civil servants, provided by Able Futures and Remploy (both links open in new tabs). You can apply to these suppliers directly for the support you need.

Other employment or self-employment

You can still use this service to apply for an Access to Work grant for:

  • self-employment
  • non-civil service employment
