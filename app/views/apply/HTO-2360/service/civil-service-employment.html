How to get support for your civil service employment

As you are a civil servant, your employer will provide support instead of Access to Work, unless you need mental health support.

You will need to contact your employer so they can assess and arrange the support you need.

If you need mental health support

You can still get Access to Work for mental health services if you are a civil servant. You can apply for support from either:

  • Able Futures
  • Remploy

These providers will contact Access to Work on your behalf. You will not need to tell your employer.
