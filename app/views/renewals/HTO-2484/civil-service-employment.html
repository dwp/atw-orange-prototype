Access to Work renewals for civil service employment

If your grant ends on or after 1 April 2022

Your employer will provide support instead of Access to Work, so you will not be able to renew your grant.

You must contact your employer so they can assess and arrange the support you need.

If your grant ends before 1 April 2022

You can still renew your Access to Work grant, as long as your grant end date is before 1 April 2022.

You can find your grant end date on your award letter or renewal reminder.
